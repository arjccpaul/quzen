QueueLess / WaitMate - Product Documentation

Tagline: Wait from anywhere, not from the line.

1. Product Overview: QueueLess is a smart virtual queue and waiting intelligence system that
allows users to join queues remotely, track live status, and receive dynamic estimated waiting times
while reducing physical crowding.

2. Core Problem: People waste hours in physical queues without knowing exact waiting time,
leading to frustration, inefficiency, and crowding in hospitals, banks, salons, and government
offices.

3. Solution: A digital queue system combined with intelligent prediction and user feedback loop to
provide accurate waiting time and remote queue management.

4. User Flow: (1) Select service → (2) Join queue → (3) Receive token + ETA → (4) Wait remotely
→ (5) Arrival notification → (6) Confirm arrival → (7) Feedback

5. Business Flow: Admin dashboard manages live queue, counters, analytics, peak hours, and
service efficiency.

6. Intelligence System: Uses historical data + live updates + user confirmations to improve ETA
accuracy similar to Google Maps/Waze.

7. Use Cases: Clinics, hospitals, salons, restaurants, banks, and government services.

8. Architecture: Mobile app, web dashboard, backend queue engine, real-time updates, database,
AI prediction layer.

9. Privacy: No continuous tracking, optional arrival detection, anonymous tokens.

10. Revenue Model: SaaS subscription for businesses, tiered plans, enterprise contracts.

11. MVP Strategy: Start with clinics and salons, expand to restaurants and banks, then government
pilots.

12. Final Positioning: QueueLess is a real-time waiting intelligence network optimizing human time
and reducing physical queues globally.
